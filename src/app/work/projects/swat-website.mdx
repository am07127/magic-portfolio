## Overview

The SWAT IOBM website was designed and developed to represent the efforts of a welfare organization working for the underprivileged. The goal was to create a highly responsive and visually engaging platform to highlight SWAT's mission, achievements, and ongoing programs, while encouraging visitors to contribute to their cause.

## Key Features

- **Clear Navigation**: Created a structured menu for effortless navigation, including sections for About, Drives, Projects, Events, and Contact.
- **Impact Highlights**: Showcased key statistics like "150+ Drives Conducted" and "10,000+ Ration Bags Delivered" to emphasize SWAT's achievements.
- **Journey Timeline**: Added a visually appealing timeline to narrate SWAT's journey and milestones since its inception.
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized the layout to ensure accessibility across devices, offering a consistent user experience on mobile and desktop.
- **Voices of Impact**: Featured testimonials from beneficiaries and volunteers to build trust and credibility.
- **Collaboration Display**: Included logos of collaborating organizations to underline the network supporting SWAT's initiatives.

## Technologies Used

- **React and Next.js**: Built using Next.js for its performance capabilities and SEO optimization.
- **Tailwind CSS**: Leveraged for styling, ensuring a clean and modern look with efficient development.
- **Framer Motion**: Added subtle animations for interactive and user-friendly engagement.
- **Email Integration**: Implemented dynamic contact forms with pre-configured email handling.

## Challenges and Learnings

While building this website, one of the major challenges was designing the timeline and "Impact Highlights" in a visually engaging manner. By iterating over multiple layouts and testing user feedback, I was able to deliver an intuitive and visually balanced design. Another learning was ensuring accessibility for a diverse audience, as the platform targets donors, volunteers, and beneficiaries.
